Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what information Lotus Hub (“we”, “us”) collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Lotus Hub you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

  • Account information you provide when you sign up — your email address and display name, or the basic profile details from Google if you sign in with Google.
  • Profile information you choose to add — profile photo, location, nationality/flag, short bio, favourite court and paddle, availability, social handle, and a self-reported skill level and DUPR rating.
  • Activity within the app — the open-play sessions you create or join and the game scores recorded for those sessions.

How we use your information

  • To run the app: sign you in, create your profile, and let you find and join games.
  • To show your profile, roster presence, and results to other signed-in players — that visibility is the core purpose of the app.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the service.

How your information is shared

  • With other players: your profile (name, photo, flag, skill, self-reported rating, and the details you add) is visible to other signed-in Lotus Hub users. Please only add information you’re comfortable sharing.
  • With our service providers: we use Google Firebase (authentication and database) to operate the app. Your data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure and processed under Google’s terms.
  • We do not sell your personal information and we don’t share it for third-party advertising.

Data retention & your choices

  • You can edit or clear most profile fields at any time from your Profile.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us (below); we’ll remove your profile and personal data within a reasonable period, except where we must retain it to comply with the law.

Children

Lotus Hub is not intended for children under 13, and we don’t knowingly collect information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we’ll remove it.
